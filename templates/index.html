About Climate Twin

Resilient Observation & Outlook Kernel (R.O.O.K)

Developed for the ISRO Hackathon, R.O.O.K is a pilot high-resolution Climate Digital Twin designed to simulate micro-climates, rainfall contours, and sea-surface anomalies across the state of Andhra Pradesh, India.
